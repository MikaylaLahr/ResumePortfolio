CSArch: Summer Internship

June 2022 - August 2022

During my summer internship at CSArch in Albany, NY I was able to explore several aspects of the company's work within the architecture, engineering, and construction management sectors. In this multi-disciplined office I was treated as a full-time fellow staff member.

I became proficient utilizing the Autodesk Revit platform to create HVAC, electrical, and plumping virtual models that were integrated with existing building conditions. The drawings and documents that I generated using Revit, were used to create BIM (Building Information Modeling) models for current projects. Other work activities included reviewing vendor components to ensure they met work project specifications which taught me the importance of accurate construction components. I was also able to participate in project management meetings with building designers, architects, and engineers on the redesign of educational facilities. I was able to learn about the various responsibilities and design ideas from the cross-functional professional team members. In addition, I gained valuable in-site on new technologies from attending lunchtime professional development seminars.

During my on-site visits to A/E projects at various stages of completion, I assisted in data collection and construction observation. These site visits gave me a better understanding of details that can affect equipment installation. Through this internship, I gained new technical skills, a working knowledge of HVAC systems, and NYS mechanical codes.
